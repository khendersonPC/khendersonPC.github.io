A new way to relax

Calm is a website that allows people to discover something new about themselves, take a breather after work, or just to have fun. Find something new, meet new people, or just head to bed. Nobody is around to judge here.

Integrating tech into relaxation

Most people don't have time to relax, but Calm is designed to solve that. By taking a short survey, Calm will find apps and websites you can relax with, to maximize the amount of stress that can be relieved from your too-short breaks.

Finding the music that was made for you

Music means something to everyone, and to many, it's an effective way to calm down. As it can be enjoyed anywhere, music has been proven as an effective stress relieiver. By linking your Spotify account to Calm, we can find the perfect album to give you the perfect chill day, whether that's means sitting on the couch playing games or hiking.

Games that will keep you up until 3:00AM

Gaming may be talked about by the media as a terrible thing, but we all know it's not. Calm finds all of the most recent stress relief games in genres like rhythm, soundtrack, adventure, and many more to make sure you have a good time and stay lost in endless worlds of possibility.
